# Tedim Chin Noun Paradigms

## Overview

Tedim Chin nouns inflect for:
- **Number**: Singular (unmarked), Plural (*-te*)
- **Case**: Absolutive (unmarked), Ergative (*-in*), Locative (*-ah*), Ablative (*-panin*), Comitative (*-tawh*)

The order of suffixes is: **STEM-PLURAL-CASE**

Example: *gam-te-ah* = land-PL-LOC "in the lands"

---

## gam "land"

| Case | Singular | Gloss | Plural | Gloss |
|------|----------|-------|--------|-------|
| Absolutive | gam | land | gam-te | land-PL |
| Ergative | gam-in | land-ERG | gam-te-in | land-PL-ERG |
| Locative | gam-ah | land-LOC | gam-te-ah | land-PL-LOC |
| Ablative | gam-panin | land-ABL | gam-te-panin | land-PL-ABL |
| Comitative | gam-tawh | land-COM | gam-te-tawh | land-PL-COM |
