# Tedim Chin Verb Stems

This report documents verb stems in Tedim Chin, organized by semantic domain.
Total verb stems in lexicon: **690**

## Overview

Tedim Chin verbs are characterized by:

1. **Stem alternation** (Form I / Form II)
   - Form I: Indicative, used in conclusive sentences
   - Form II: Subjunctive, used in subordinate/inconclusive clauses
   - Changes: add -h/-k, tone change, -ng → -n

2. **Pronominal prefixes** for subject agreement
   - ka- (1SG), na- (2SG), a- (3SG), i- (1PL.INCL)

3. **TAM suffixes** for tense-aspect-mood
   - -ding (IRR), -ta (PFV), -zo (COMPL), -kik (ITER)

4. **Derivational morphology**
   - ki- (reflexive/middle), -sak (causative), -pih (applicative)
