# Tedim Chin Coordinators

This report documents the coordination system of Tedim Chin based on analysis
of the Bible corpus (830,741 tokens).

## Overview

Tedim Chin uses different coordinators for:
- **NP coordination**: le "and"
- **Clause coordination**: leh, a "and", ahih hangin "but"
- **Disjunction**: mawh "or"

The primary distinction is between **le** (NP-level) and **leh** (clause-level).

## NP Coordination: le

The coordinator **le** joins noun phrases:

**Structure**: NP₁ le NP₂

**Genesis 1:1**:
> Pasian in vantung **le** leitung a piangsak hi
> *god ERG heaven and earth 3SG create DECL*
> "God created the heaven **and** the earth"

### Common Patterns

| Pattern | Meaning | Frequency |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| pa le nu | father and mother | common |
| mipa le numei | male and female | common |
| ni le zan | day and night | common |
| vantung le leitung | heaven and earth | 6+ |

### Multiple Conjuncts

For more than two NPs, **le** chains:

> A **le** B **le** C
> "A and B and C"

---

## Clause Coordination: leh

The coordinator **leh** joins clauses and can have conditional meaning:

**Structure**: CLAUSE₁ leh CLAUSE₂

| Meaning | Count |
|---------|-------|
| "and (clause)" | common |
| "if, then" | common |

### As Conjunction "and"

When **leh** simply conjoins:

> pai leh en in
> *go CONJ see IMP.SG*
> "go and see"

### As Conditional "if"

**leh** often introduces conditional clauses:

> tua bawl leh pha ding hi
> *DIST do if good IRR DECL*
> "if (you) do that, it will be good"

---

## Sequential Coordination: a

The particle **a** (often as 3SG agreement) chains sequential events:

| Form | Function | Count |
|------|----------|-------|
| a | sequential coordinator | 78,120 |

**Genesis 1:3**:
> Pasian in "Khuavak om ta hen" **a** ci **a**, khuavak om ta hi
> *god ERG light exist PFV OPT 3SG say and light exist PFV DECL*
> "And God said, Let there be light: **and** there was light"

### With Demonstratives

Sequential chaining often uses **a, tua**:

| Pattern | Meaning | Count |
|---------|---------|-------|
| a, tua | "and, that" | 877 |
| tua ciangin | "then, at that time" | 4,305 |

---

## Adversative: ahih hangin

Marks contrast or concession ("but", "however"):

| Form | Gloss | Count |
|------|-------|-------|
| ahih hangin | but, however | 1,422 |

**Structure**: CLAUSE₁, ahih hangin CLAUSE₂

**Genesis 3:4**:
> **Ahih hangin** gulpi in numei kiangah, "Na si kei ding uh hi"
> *but serpent ERG woman to-LOC 2SG die NEG IRR 2/3PL DECL*
> "**But** the serpent said unto the woman, Ye shall not die"

### Related Forms

| Form | Meaning | Count |
|------|---------|-------|
| ahih kei leh | "otherwise, if not" | 203 |
| ahih ciangin | "being so, then" | 1,507 |

---

## Disjunction: mawh

Marks alternative ("or"):

| Form | Gloss | Count |
|------|-------|-------|
| mawh | or | 144 |

**Structure**: X mawh Y "X or Y"

**Example**:
> mi mawh ganhing mawh
> *person or animal or*
> "person or animal"

### Alternative Questions

**mawh** can form alternative questions:

> pai ding **mawh** om ding **mawh**
> "will (you) go or will (you) stay?"

---

## Coordination vs. Subordination

Tedim Chin has a gradient between coordination and subordination:

| Marker | Level | Function |
|--------|-------|----------|
| le | NP | pure coordination |
| leh | clause | coordination/conditional |
| a | clause | sequential chaining |
| ciangin | clause | temporal subordination |
| hangin | clause | causal/adversative |

### Clause Chaining

Many "coordinated" clauses use converb structure (see 8-clause-02):

> VERB₁-in VERB₂-in VERB₃ hi
> "having V₁, having V₂, V₃"

This is technically subordination but functions as narrative sequencing.

## Word Order

### NP Coordination

> **[NP₁ le NP₂]** VERB
> "NP₁ and NP₂ VERB"

### Clause Coordination

> **CLAUSE₁**, leh/a **CLAUSE₂**
> "CLAUSE₁, and/then CLAUSE₂"

### Adversative

> **CLAUSE₁**, ahih hangin **CLAUSE₂**
> "CLAUSE₁, but CLAUSE₂"

**Key distinction**:
- **le** = NP-level "and"
- **leh** = clause-level "and/if"
- **ahih hangin** = "but" (adversative)
- **mawh** = "or" (disjunction)

**Total coordinator tokens**: ~15,000+ (1.8% of corpus)
