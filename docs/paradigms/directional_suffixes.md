# Directional Suffixes in Tedim Chin

Directional suffixes indicate the path or direction of motion associated with the verbal action.

## Inventory

| Suffix | Gloss | Description | Attestations |
|--------|-------|-------------|--------------|
| -khia | out | Outward - motion out of | 1,006 |
| -khiat | away | Away - motion away from | 180 |
| -toh | up | Upward - motion up | 39 |
| -lam | DIR | Direction - toward, manner | 24 |
| -sawn | toward | Toward - motion toward | 13 |
| -lut | in | Inward - motion into | 0 |
| -cip | down | Downward - motion down | 0 |
| -tang | arrive | Arrive - reach endpoint | 0 |
