# Tedim Chin Morpheme Inventory

Based on analysis of the Tedim Bible corpus and Henderson 1965.

## Notes on Orthography

1. Apostrophe (') marks syllable boundary or glottal stop
2. Final -h indicates low/falling tone in some transcriptions
3. Hyphen joins clitics (ci-in, mai-ah)

## Sample Glossed Sentence

**Mark 1:1**: Pasian' Tapa Jesuh Khrih tawh kisai Lungdamna Thu hih bang ahi hi.

```
Pasian'    Tapa   Jesuh  Khrih   tawh  kisai      Lungdamna     Thu   hih   bang   ahi     hi
God.GEN    son    Jesus  Christ  COM   concern    gospel        word  PROX  what   be.3SG  DECL
"This is what the gospel about Jesus Christ, the Son of God, is."
```
