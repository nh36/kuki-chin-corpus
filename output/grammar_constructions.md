# Tedim Chin Constructions

This report documents grammatical constructions in Tedim Chin,
organized by category with pattern descriptions and corpus examples.

---

## Agreement

### Subject Agreement

**Pattern:** `PRO-V`

Pronominal prefixes (ka-, na-, a-, i-, ei-) mark subject agreement.

**Frequency:** ~200,000

**Morphemes:** -a (3SG), -ka (1SG), -na (2SG)

**Examples:**

**(01 1:1)**
> A kipat cil-in Pasian in vantung le leitung a piangsak hi.
> *'In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth.'*

**(01 1:4)**
> Pasian in tua khuavak hoih hi, ci-in a mu hi. Pasian in khuamial panin khuavak khenkhia hi.
> *'And God saw the light, that it was good: and God divided the light from the dark...'*

**(01 1:5)**
> Pasian in khuavak pen “Sun” ci a, khuamial pen “Zan” ci hi. Nitak hong bei-in, zingsang hong tung a,...
> *'And God called the light Day, and the darkness he called Night. And the evening ...'*


### Inverse Agreement

**Pattern:** `hong-V / kong-V`

Inverse/directional prefixes (hong-, kong-) mark non-canonical agent-patient relations.

**Frequency:** ~25,000


### Plural Agreement

**Pattern:** `V uh`

Plural suffix -uh marks 2nd/3rd person plural agreement.

**Frequency:** ~15,000

**Morphemes:** -uh (2/3PL)

**Examples:**

**(24 47:6)**
> Note in, ' Topa' namsau aw, tawlnga nai lo ding na hi hiam? Na paai sungah lutkik inla, taang loin k...
> *'O thou sword of the LORD , how long will it be ere thou be quiet? put up thyself...'*

**(40 8:29)**
> Amaute nakpitakin hong kiko uh a, “Pasian' Tapa aw, kote bang hong nuammawh na hi hiam? A hun tung n...
> *'And, behold, they cried out, saying, What have we to do with thee, Jesus, thou S...'*

**(04 9:3)**
> A kiseh hun, tu kha ni sawm le li ni nitakin a thukhamte le a ngeina kip omte bangin na kham ding uh...
> *'In the fourteenth day of this month, at even, ye shall keep it in his appointed ...'*

## Aspect Construction

### Perfective Aspect

**Pattern:** `V-ta`

Marks completed action with -ta (PFV). Past or anterior reference.

**Frequency:** ~25,000

**Morphemes:** -ta (PFV)

**Examples:**

**(01 5:19)**
> Enok' suah khit ciangin Jared kum zagiat nungta a, tanu le tapa dang nei lai hi.
> *'and Jared lived after he begat Enoch eight hundred years, and begat sons and dau...'*

**(01 5:7)**
> Enosh a suah khit ciangin Seth kum zagiat le kum sawmthum nungta a, tanu le tapa dang nei lai hi.
> *'and Seth lived after he begat Enos eight hundred and seven years, and begat sons...'*

**(01 5:13)**
> Mahalalel' suah khit ciangin Kenan kum zagiat le kum sawmli nungta a, tanu le tapa dang nei lai hi.
> *'and Cainan lived after he begat Mahalaleel eight hundred and forty years, and be...'*


### Completive Aspect

**Pattern:** `V-zo`

Marks completed action with -zo (COMPL). Emphasizes completion.

**Frequency:** ~8,000

**Morphemes:** -zo (COMPL)

**Examples:**

**(01 41:41)**
> Faro in Josef' kiangah, “Egypt gam khempeuh a uk dingin nang kong koih khinzo hi,” a ci hi.
> *'And Pharaoh said unto Joseph, See, I have set thee over all the land of Egypt.'*

**(01 31:9)**
> Tua ahih ciangin Pasian in na pa' ganhing khempeuh la khia-in tuate keima tungah hong piazo hi.
> *'Thus God hath taken away the cattle of your father, and given them to me.'*

**(01 45:6)**
> Bang hang hiam cih leh gam sungah kum nih sung kialpi tungzo hi. Lo khawh loh, an lak loh ding kum n...
> *'For these two years hath the famine been in the land: and yet there are five yea...'*


### Continuative Aspect

**Pattern:** `V-lai`

Marks ongoing/continuous action with -lai (CONT). "still"

**Frequency:** ~5,000


### Sequential Aspect

**Pattern:** `V-khit`

Marks sequential action with -khit (SEQ). "after V-ing"

**Frequency:** ~4,000

**Morphemes:** -khit (SEQ)

**Examples:**

**(43 5:6)**

> *'When Jesus saw him lie, and knew that he had been now a long time in that case ,...'*

> Tu-in na khempeuh na theihkhitna thu ka phawk uh a, ka theihnop uh thute kote in ka hong dot ding uh...
> *'Now are we sure that thou knowest all things, and needest not that any man shoul...'*

**(44 24:10)**
> Paul in thu a gen nadingin menzipa in a khut tawh a lah ciangin Paul in, “Hih minamte tungah thukhen...
> *'Then Paul, after that the governor had beckoned unto him to speak, answered, For...'*


### Iterative Aspect

**Pattern:** `V-kik`

Marks repeated/returned action with -kik (ITER). "again, back"

**Frequency:** ~3,500

**Morphemes:** -kik (ITER)

**Examples:**

**(07 11:14)**
> Jefthah in Ammon mite' kumpipa kiangah kamtaite sawlkikin
> *'And Jephthah sent messengers again unto the king of the children of Ammon:'*

**(03 25:24)**
> “Note in na neih uh leitang khempeuh na kitanhkiksak thei ding uh hi.
> *'And in all the land of your possession ye shall grant a redemption for the land.'*

**(03 25:26)**
> Mi khat in tua na a tatsak ding mi a neih kei a, amahmah hong haukikin a tanhzo dingin a pian' leh,
> *'And if the man have none to redeem it, and himself be able to redeem it;'*

## Case Construction

### Ergative Agent Marking

**Pattern:** `NP-ERG V`

Marks agent of transitive verb with -in (ERG). Tedim shows ergative-absolutive alignment.

**Frequency:** ~77,717

**Morphemes:** -in (ERG)

**Examples:**

**(01 1:1)**
> A kipat cil-in Pasian in vantung le leitung a piangsak hi.
> *'In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth.'*

**(01 1:13)**
> Nitak hong bei-in zingsang hong tung a, ni thum ni ahi hi.
> *'And the evening and the morning were the third day.'*

**(01 1:3)**
> Pasian in, “Khuavak om hen,” ci hi; tua ciangin khuavak om pah hi.
> *'And God said, Let there be light: and there was light.'*

## Clause Final

### Declarative Clause

**Pattern:** `CLAUSE hi`

Declarative sentence-final marker hi (DECL). Asserts proposition.

**Frequency:** ~47,720

**Morphemes:** -hi (DECL)

**Examples:**

**(19 118:3)**
> “Ama itna kip tawntung hi,” Aaron innkuante in ci ta uh hen.
> *'Let the house of Aaron now say, That his mercy endureth for ever.'*

**(19 34:22)**
> Topa in ama nasemte honkhia a, amah a bel mi khat beek gim kipia lo ding hi.
> *'The LORD redeemeth the soul of his servants: And none of them that trust in him ...'*

**(20 23:30)**
> Zu ne-a a tut khote zu a nenete ahi hi.
> *'They that tarry long at the wine; They that go to seek mixed wine.'*
